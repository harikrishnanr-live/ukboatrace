About Upper Kuttanad Boat Race

The Upper Kuttanad Boat Race is one of the most anticipated and celebrated events in Kerala, India. It is a vibrant display of the state's rich cultural heritage, maritime traditions, and community spirit. The race features the iconic snake boats (Chundan Vallam) and other traditional boats, each manned by teams of skilled oarsmen who compete with great fervor and enthusiasm.

The event is not just a race, but a grand festival that attracts thousands of spectators from all over the world. The backwaters of Kuttanad provide a stunning backdrop for the race, making it a visual treat for all who attend.

The race is organized by a dedicated committee of local leaders and volunteers who work tirelessly to ensure the event's success. The Upper Kuttanad Boat Race is a testament to the unity, skill, and passion of the people of Kerala.
