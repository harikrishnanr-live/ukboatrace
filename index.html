About the Race

The Upper Kuttanad Boat Race is a celebration of Kerala's rich maritime heritage. Held annually on the serene backwaters of Kuttanad, this event brings together the finest snake boats and their skilled oarsmen for a spectacle of speed, synchrony, and tradition. The race is not just a competition, but a grand festival that showcases the spirit and culture of Kerala.
