Participating Boats & Captains

Chundan Vallam

Captain: Rajesh Kumar

Club: Kuttanad Rowing Club

Wins: 5

Chundan Vallam, also known as snake boats, are the stars of Kerala's boat races. These long, narrow boats can accommodate up to 100 rowers and are a symbol of Kerala's maritime heritage.

Veppu Vallam

Captain: Anil Kumar

Club: Alappuzha Boat Club

Wins: 3

Veppu Vallam are known for their speed and agility. These boats are slightly smaller than Chundan Vallam but are equally competitive and thrilling to watch.
